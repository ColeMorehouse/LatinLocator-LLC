LatinLocator



A Latin language academic service designed for the 21st century


        Salve Amici! LatinLocator is a proprietary research tool and study aid designed to help Latin students and researchers alike gain a stronger grasp on the Latin language through relevant content aggregation and immersive learning technologies. Using the vast connectivity of the internet and the recent advances in e-learning technologies, we hope to supplement your Latin education and provide you with the tools to ace your tests and gain a deeper understanding and appreciation for the classical world.

What is LatinLocator?

        LatinLocator is an intelligent content aggregation tool that compresses your piles of papers and texts into one slick online heads–up–display. Using an advanced yet intuitive searching tool, a single query of a phrase, name, line, or text will search our databases and quickly return to you the most relevant information in a readable format. With many customizable features and a modern GUI, LatinLocator operates as your personal assistant for classical studies!

This is how it works – enter a Latin phrase and LatinLocator automatically provides:
  • Complete Latin passage â€“ text before and after the entered Latin phrase (which is shown in bold)
  • Identifies the author and Latin document in which the phrase appears, such as the book, poem, story, etc.
  • Translates the passage into English, both literally and figuratively
  • Provides links to commentaries explaining the Latin passage, including context, relevance, etc.

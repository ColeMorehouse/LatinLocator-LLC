Jon Merril

CEO

Jonathan Merril is a serial entrepreneur, who has founded and managed a number of educational technology companies that resulted in both public and provide acquisitions. Jon has focused his career on the use of technologies that include simulation, machine learning and cloud–based solutions to catalyze business and IP growth. Jon is also an expert in the use of intellectual property and data analytics to drive value by augmenting R&D efforts, defending technology positioning, and identifying present and future competitors, customers, M&A candidates, etc. Successful exits include the sale of HT Medical (medical virtual reality education company) to Immersion Corp (NASDAQ); C3P Pharmacy Education (pharmacy education platform) to HealthAnswers, Inc.; and Astute Tech (e–learning platform) to Echo360.

This is how it works – enter a Latin phrase and LatinLocator automatically provides:
  • Complete Latin passage – text before and after the entered Latin phrase (which is shown in bold)
  • Identifies the author and Latin document in which the phrase appears, such as the book, poem, story, etc.
  • Translates the passage into English, both literally and figuratively
  • Provides links to commentaries explaining the Latin passage, including context, relevance, etc.

A Latin language study and teaching aid designed for the 21st century
Salve Amici!


       LatinLocator is a proprietary research tool and study aid designed to help Latin students and researchers alike gain a stronger grasp of the Latin language through relevant content aggregation and immersive learning technologies. Using the vast connectivity of the internet and recent advances in e–learning technologies, LatinLocator is able to supplement text books and other resources to enhance your knowledge and improve test scores – and importantly facilitate a deeper understanding and appreciation for classical Latin.

A unique tool tailored to the needs of Latin students and scholars


        By providing students and scholars with easily accessible, relevant information covering over 9000 individual lines of written Latin, LatinLocator uncovers the path to a truly deeper understanding of Latin through the digital world. Whether you are using our tool to expedite academic research or to improve performance on your next Latin test, Latin Locator will help you to achieve your goals.
